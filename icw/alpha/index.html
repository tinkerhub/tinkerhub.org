Beyond the playbook

A talk by a founder or thought leader who will inspire students to think beyond the textbook and build innovative products or acquire skills that will help them grow.

Retrospective - IEDC / Campus Innovation

Students will be asked to identify key challenges with respect to their Innovation units and campuses, and prioritize the challenges through voting. After the voting, the most voted challenges will be discussed among students, and they would be asked to come up with solutions for the same.

Grooming - Future of IEDC

Grooming the solutions suggested by students. This will be a more detailed deliberation regarding possible solutions and ideas, and on how to implement it in campuses.

Future of learning

Focuses on the importance of peer to peer learning in campus life and how to improve skill set that leads to innovations in the campus. Storytelling by a successful innovator to follow.

Future Technology

Introducing 3 technologies/areas that students should focus on for next one year - Machine Learning, Hardware Hacking and Kotlin.

Learn & Hack

Students will get the chance to learn a new technology in a few hours.
